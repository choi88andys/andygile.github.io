Privacy Policy

MMpedia ("Service") provides murder mystery theme reviews, party matching, and deposit escrow. Personal data is processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including Korea's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account: email, password (hashed), nickname
  • Social sign-in: email, provider identifiers (Apple, Google, Kakao)
  • Service usage: reviews, ratings, party participation and booking history
  • Payments: deposit transaction records (card data held by PG only, never stored by the Service)
  • Automatic: IP address, device identifier, login attempt logs, push notification device token

2. Purpose of Collection

  • Member identification, authentication, and account management
  • Party matching, reservations, and payment mediation
  • Abuse prevention and security audit logging
  • Announcements and customer support

3. Retention Period

  • Account information: until membership withdrawal
  • Payment and booking records: 5 years (per Korean e-commerce law)
  • Login logs: 3 months (per Korean telecommunications privacy law)

4. Processors

The Service does not share personal information with third parties without consent. The following processors receive the minimum data required to operate the Service:

  • Supabase Inc. (database and authentication infrastructure)
  • Apple, Google, Kakao (social sign-in)
  • Resend (transactional email delivery)
  • Cloudflare (bot protection and network security)
  • PG provider (payment processing — planned for V1.1)

5. Your Rights

You may at any time request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. Account deletion is available via "Settings > Account > Delete Account" within the app, or by emailing the address below.

6. Security Measures

  • Passwords stored as one-way hashes (bcrypt)
  • SSL/TLS encryption in transit
  • Row-Level Security on all database tables
  • Breached-password screening (HIBP pre-check)
  • Least-privilege access and audit logging

7. Data Protection Contact

For any data protection questions, please contact us at andy@andygile.com.
