Features

Everything you need for your intermittent fasting journey.

Accurate Timer

Your fasting timer keeps perfect time, even if you close the app or restart your phone.

Track every fast with a calendar view. See your completed fasts, streaks, and patterns.

Dive into weekly and monthly charts, streak tracking, average duration, and completion rates.
