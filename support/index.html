ShalloWeDive

ShalloWeDive works on iPhone and Apple Watch. The Apple Watch is required for underwater depth and temperature tracking during dives.
ShalloWeDive uses a 4-level safety zone system based on AIDA International guidelines. The zones are determined by your depth and dive duration, providing real-time visual feedback on your safety status.
Yes. All your dive data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or share any personal or health data.

Fasting Timer

Fasting Timer offers four plans: 16:8 (free), 18:6, 20:4, and OMAD (23:1). The 16:8 plan is available to all users; other plans require a Premium subscription.
Yes. The timer uses your fasting start time to calculate elapsed time, so it stays accurate even if you close the app, restart your phone, or switch devices.
Premium unlocks all fasting plans (18:6, 20:4, OMAD), detailed statistics and charts, home screen and lock screen widgets, and priority support. Subscriptions come with a 3-day free trial.
