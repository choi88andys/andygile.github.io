Privacy Policy

ShalloWeDive ("Service") can be used without signing in and does not collect or transmit any user information to external servers.

The Service processes the following data exclusively on-device and does not transmit it externally:

  • Health Data (Heart Rate): Heart rate is displayed during dives via Apple HealthKit. Data is stored only on the user's device (Apple Watch/iPhone).
  • Fitness Data (Dive Workouts): Dive sessions are recorded as HealthKit workouts. Data is stored only on the user's device.
  • Environment Data (Depth, Water Temperature): Collected via the Apple Watch Ultra depth sensor for dive safety metrics. Data is stored only on the user's device.

All data is processed exclusively on-device through Apple's HealthKit and CoreMotion frameworks. No data is transmitted to the developer or any third party.
