Features

Everything you need for safe and informed freediving.

Depth Tracking

Real-time depth monitoring directly on your Apple Watch during every dive.

Water Temperature

Monitor water temperature changes throughout your dive session.

Heart Rate Monitoring

Keep track of your heart rate to stay aware of your body's condition.

AIDA Safety Standards

Built-in safety protocols following AIDA International guidelines.

4-Level Safety Zones

Visual indicators guide you through four safety zones based on depth and duration.

Rest Timer

Automated rest interval timing to ensure proper recovery between dives.

Ascent Speed

Real-time ascent rate monitoring to prevent unsafe surfacing speeds.
